About us

How we met.

  • 2010

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Pedro set eyes on Lucy when she arrived for a job interview at his place of work. Lucy wondered why he was staring at her. Lucy got the job and after many failed attempts, she finally agreed to go for an after work drink with him. Pedro later found out she only agreed because she had nothing better to do that night… that was nice Lucy!

  • March 2011

    Meet the Rathbornes

    Pedro did not know when he was going to meet Lucy’s parents, neither did they! Lucy decided to quickly introduce everyone before leaving to swim around The London Docklands one bright, sunny, summers afternoon.

  • December 2012

    Mount View

    Lucy moved into Pedro’s shared house in North London while training to become a Speech and Language Therapist. During this time, Pedro refined his culinary skills in an extreme effort to please Lucy who hated sitting at her desk studying all the time. At weekends, they’d brunch and blow the cobwebs away on their matching bikes, a habit they’re yet to grow out of.

  • Feb 2017

    Shiny

    Pedro finally proposed to Lucy up in the clouds of Table Mountain in Cape Town on the 5th May 2017. Pedro didn’t get much of a reaction initially, as Lucy couldn’t quite process what had happened until 20 minutes later! She then became extremely excited suggesting they go for steak and red wine to celebrate.

Getting here

  • The closest airport is Cork (ORK) which is about 80kms from Skibbereen. Carriers are RyanAir & Aer-Lingus and is a short 45 minute flight from most UK airports.

  • Skibbereen is about 1h20 drive from Cork airport. Car hire is recommended as transport is expensive and you will need to get around the town and venue. You can take the N75 westbound and the roads are fairly well signposted.
