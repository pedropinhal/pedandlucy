How we met

The story till now.

  • One Monday morning...

    Pedro first noticed Lucy as she arrived for a job interview at his place of work. Lucy on the other hand wondered why Pedro was staring at her. She got the job and after many failed attempts, finally agreed to a drink. Pedro later found out Lucy only joined him, because she had nothing better to do that night… that was nice Lucy!

  • Meet the parents

    Pedro did not know when he was going to meet Lucy’s parents, neither did they. Lucy decided to quickly introduce everyone before leaving to swim around The London Docklands one bright, sunny, summers afternoon.

  • Mount View Road, N4

    Lucy moved into Pedro’s shared house in North London while training to become a Speech and Language Therapist. During this time, Pedro refined his culinary skills in an effort to please Lucy who hated sitting at her desk studying. At weekends, they’d brunch and blow the cobwebs away on their matching bikes, a habit they’re yet to grow out of.

  • Shiny

    Pedro finally proposed to Lucy up in the clouds of Table Mountain in Cape Town on the 5th May 2017. Pedro didn’t get much of a reaction initially, as Lucy couldn’t quite process what had happened until 20 minutes later! She then became extremely excited suggesting they go for steak and red wine to celebrate.

Getting here

  • The closest airport is Cork Airport, 80km from Skibbereen. Cork Airport is a short, 50 minute flight from most UK airports. Ryanair and Aer-Lingus operate frequent direct flights. Please book your flights early to avoid high-cost fares. View more here Google Flights.

  • We would recommend hiring a car, as transport options to Skibbereen are limited and travelling by car is the easiest way to see Skibbereen and it's surrounding areas. If you book early, car hire is very, very inexpensive. Cork Airport have a good list of car hire companies.
